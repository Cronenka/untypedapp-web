Frequently asked questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the menu, go to Account, then select Delete Account and confirm. This permanently removes your data.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play Store, not within the app. Go to your device's subscription settings to cancel or change your plan.

Is my voice data stored?

Your recordings are processed to generate your email or note and are not retained longer than necessary to deliver that result. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Which languages are supported?

Untyped currently supports English, with additional languages on the way.

The app crashed or something isn't working — what should I do?

Email us at untypedapp@gmail.com with a short description of what happened and your device model, and we'll help you sort it out.
