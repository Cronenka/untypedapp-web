Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Untyped ("the app") is operated by Cronenka. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data we collect

We collect and store your email address when you connect a Gmail or Outlook account. This is used to restore your account and address book after reinstall and manage your subscription status.

Voice recordings

Voice recordings are processed in real time and never stored on our servers. Audio is sent to Deepgram for transcription and immediately discarded. See Deepgram's Privacy Policy for details.

Email content

Email content is generated using Anthropic Claude and is never stored on our servers. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details.

Gmail access

When you connect your Gmail account, we request permission to send emails on your behalf and, for Pro subscribers, to read your inbox via the Gmail API (gmail.readonly scope). Inbox access is used solely to display your emails within the Voice Inbox feature so you can reply by voice. We do not store, share, sell, or transmit your email content to any third party. Email content is only accessed while you are actively using the Voice Inbox feature and is never written to our servers. You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account settings.

Outlook access

When you connect your Outlook account, we request permission to send emails on your behalf using the Microsoft Graph Mail.Send scope and, for Pro subscribers, to read your inbox via the Mail.Read scope. Inbox access is used solely to display your emails within the Voice Inbox feature so you can reply by voice. We do not store, share, sell, or transmit your email content to any third party. Email content is only accessed while you are actively using the Voice Inbox feature and is never written to our servers. You can revoke access at any time from your Microsoft Account settings. See Microsoft's Privacy Statement for details.

Address book

If you save contacts to your in-app address book, those contacts (name and email address) are stored securely on our servers and linked to your account email. This allows your address book to be restored if you reinstall the app. You can delete individual contacts within the app, or request full deletion by contacting us.

Usage data

We use Supabase to store your email address and address book. This is used solely to restore your account after reinstall and manage subscription status. See Supabase's Privacy Policy for details.

Third-party services

The app uses the following third-party services: Deepgram (transcription), Anthropic Claude (email generation), Google Sign-In (authentication), Microsoft Identity Platform (Outlook authentication), Supabase (data storage), RevenueCat (subscription management), Sentry (crash reporting), PostHog (analytics).

Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights or request deletion of your account data, contact us at untypedapp@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Contact

For any privacy questions, contact untypedapp@gmail.com.
