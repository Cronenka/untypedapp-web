Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Untyped ("the app") is operated by Cronenka. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data we collect

We collect only a anonymised identifier (your Google account ID) and a count of emails generated. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal information.

Voice recordings

Voice recordings are processed in real time and never stored on our servers. Audio is sent to Deepgram for transcription and immediately discarded. See Deepgram's Privacy Policy for details.

Email content

Email content is generated using Anthropic Claude and is never stored on our servers. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details.

Gmail access

If you connect your Gmail account, we request permission only to send emails on your behalf. We never read, store, or access your inbox or existing emails. You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account settings.

Third-party services

The app uses the following third-party services: Deepgram (transcription), Anthropic Claude (email generation), Google Sign-In (authentication), RevenueCat (subscription management).

Your rights

You can request deletion of your usage data at any time by contacting us at privacy@untypedapp.com.

Contact

For any privacy questions, contact privacy@untypedapp.com.
